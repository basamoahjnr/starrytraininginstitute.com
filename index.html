Starry Training Institute

Starry Training Institute is a leading training company, offering Industrial Heavy Duty lifting equipment and Health & Safety training in Africa. We have trained a variety of Operators on lifting equipment.
Our major training contracts have included companies such as AngloGold Ashanti, Ghana Gold Field to name but a few. In 2019 the Institute began training in Ghana, and has since worked with up to 150+ Operators on equipment ranging from Fork Lifts, Excavators, Bulldozers, Rollers and many more.
We are able to offer a unique training experience at a reasonable price, which is conducted in English & local Ghanaian languages.
We also offer Certification to all our delegates. The benefits of our training can be seen in terms of productivity improvement, and the high morale that employees exhibit at their work places.

In Ghana we are privileged to be associated with major companies such as Gold Fields Ghana Limited, Underground Mining Alliance (AMA) and many more. Starry Training Institute is a registered Ghanaian institution that takes importance of Health and Safety Training in Mining and Manufacturing Industries seriously. Lifting Equipment has taken over much of the work which has in the past been carried out by manual labour therefore in order to prevent further accidents involving lifting equipment, a structured training programme is necessary. Mining Industries in Ghana thus are legally required to train individuals who they employ on lifting equipment, under Ghana's Mining regulations. It is therefore paramount that knowledge of safety rules is on its own not sufficient to qualify anyone to operate a Heavy Duty Equipments.
